Intermediate

A classic foundation for aspiring engineers and IT professionals, focusing on Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.

  • Science (BPC)

The pathway for future doctors and medical professionals, with core subjects including Biology, Physics, and Chemistry.

Degree

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.)

A four-year professional course for those aiming for a career in engineering, with specializations like Computer Science, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering.

  • Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.)

A three-year degree that provides a strong understanding of accounting, finance, and business management. It's a stepping stone for careers in banking, auditing, and corporate finance.

  • Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.)

A versatile program focusing on core sciences, which can lead to further research or employment in fields like data analysis, research, and scientific journalism.

Post-Graduation

  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)

A highly sought-after professional degree for career advancement in leadership and management. Specializations can include Marketing, Finance, and Human Resources.

  • Master of Technology (M.Tech)

An advanced degree for engineering graduates, focusing on in-depth knowledge and research in a specific engineering field, such as Artificial Intelligence or Machine Learning

  • Master of Science (M.Sc.)

A postgraduate program that provides deep specialization in a scientific discipline, preparing students for research, academia, or advanced roles in their respective fields.
